Professional checklist and client intake system for premium web development projects

📝 Development Checklist

1. Initial Information

  • Company / Brand name
  • Contact details
  • Business goals for the website
  • Main competitors / inspirations

2. Scope & Structure

  • Number of pages
  • Desired sections (Home, About, Services, Contact, Blog, etc.)
  • Extra features (forms, e-commerce, booking, chat, etc.)

3. Design Preferences

  • Logo and brand colours
  • Fonts
  • Style references (links or screenshots)

4. Content

  • Text content ready or to be created
  • Images / videos supplied or to be sourced
  • Language(s)

5. Integrations

  • Social media links
  • Payment gateway
  • CRM / email marketing tools

6. Timeline & Budget

  • Target launch date
  • Stages of delivery
  • Payment milestones

7. Feedback & Approval

  • Who will review each stage
  • Deadlines for feedback
